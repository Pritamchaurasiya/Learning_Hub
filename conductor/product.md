# Product Guide

## 1. Vision

To create a comprehensive educational platform, similar to Coursera and Udemy, designed to assist students in their learning journey. The platform will provide study help and offer a marketplace for selling courses, materials, and tests.

## 2. Target Audience

*   **Students:** Seeking to supplement their education, learn new skills, or prepare for exams.
*   **Instructors/Creators:** Looking to monetize their knowledge by creating and selling educational content.

## 3. Key Features

*   **Course Marketplace:** A central hub for users to browse, purchase, and enroll in courses.
*   **Study Aids:** Tools and resources to help students with their studies, such as flashcards, quizzes, and study guides.
*   **Content Creation Tools:** An interface for instructors to create and upload their courses, materials, and tests.
*   **User Profiles:** Separate profiles for students and instructors to track their progress, purchases, and earnings.

## 4. Monetization

*   **Course Sales:** A commission-based model on the sale of courses and other educational materials.
*   **Subscription Model:** Potential for a subscription service for access to premium content or features.
