## 2024-05-23 - Authentication Forms
**Learning:** Flutter's `TextInputAction` and `AutofillHints` are critical for mobile form usability. Without them, users have to manually dismiss keyboard or navigate fields. Adding `TextInputType.emailAddress` also ensures the @ symbol is readily available.
**Action:** Always verify keyboard flow on forms. Ensure "Next" moves focus and "Done/Go" submits the form.
